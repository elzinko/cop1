# Story E8-S6: Retrospective Ceremony

Status: ready-for-dev

## Story
As a team lead
I want an automated Retrospective ceremony that analyzes sprint outcomes
so that the team can identify improvements and produce actionable proposals

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Retrospective ceremony is triggered at sprint end
2. Ceremony analyzes sprint metrics and story completions
3. Team agents discuss what went well and what to improve
4. Ceremony identifies patterns and root causes
5. Team reaches consensus on improvement proposals
6. Ceremony produces retrospective summary and action items
