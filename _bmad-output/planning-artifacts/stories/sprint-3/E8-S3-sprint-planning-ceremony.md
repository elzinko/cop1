# Story E8-S3: Sprint Planning Ceremony

Status: ready-for-dev

## Story
As a sprint planner
I want an automated Sprint Planning ceremony that selects ready stories for the sprint
so that sprint scope is determined through team discussion and consensus

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Sprint Planning ceremony is triggered for upcoming sprint
2. Ceremony presents available ready stories to the team
3. Team agents discuss story relevance and capacity
4. Ceremony selects stories reaching consensus
5. Selected stories are assigned to sprint
6. Ceremony produces sprint scope and capacity plan
