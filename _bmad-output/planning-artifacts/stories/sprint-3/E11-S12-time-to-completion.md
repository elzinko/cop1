# Story E11-S12: Time-to-Completion Estimator

Status: ready-for-dev

## Story
As a sprint planner
I want a TimeEstimator component that predicts story completion timing
so that I can identify at-risk stories and adjust sprint plans proactively

## Acceptance Criteria
1. TimeEstimator analyzes story metrics and progress patterns
2. Estimator calculates projected completion time for each story
3. Estimator flags stories at risk of missing sprint deadline
4. Estimator considers velocity history and complexity factors
5. Estimates update dynamically as sprint progresses
6. Dashboard displays at-risk flag prominently for visibility
